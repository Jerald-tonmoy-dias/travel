Located in the Indian Ocean, The Maldives is a dream destination. The mention of Maldives calls to mind visions of an idyllic secluded island paradise, tropical, lush islands, and shimmering turquoise waters and is a place where dreams meet reality. Nowhere on earth can boost sea waters as clear as the Maldives. Scattered across a huge swathe of the Indian Ocean, the Maldives comprises countless strings of tiny islands, only two hundred of which are inhabited.

With good weather throughout the year, Maldives makes a great choice for an ultimate tropical island getaway. It is the ultimate dream of luxury and tranquility. The Maldives is the perfect destination for those who love the ocean and want to experience a true ‘away-from-it-all stay in paradise
